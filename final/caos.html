The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina

The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina is a supernatural horror television series developed for Netflix. CAOS is set in the fictional town of Greendale. Sabrina Spellman must come to terms with her half-witch, half-mortal nature while fighting the evil that threatens her family, herself, and the mundane world.
I have kept up with this show since it came out in 2018. I was a bit apprehensive at first as it is a show aimed towards teens. However, I was pleasantly surprised by how fun it is. It is very different from the show that many of us grew up watching, Sabrina the Teenage Witch. CAOS is dark both in color palette and themes. I would recommend that anyone with a taste for the occult watch! The final season will be released on December 31, 2020. I am looking forward to how this series will end.
-Vanessa Garcia
