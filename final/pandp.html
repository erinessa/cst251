Pride and Prejudice

Pride and Prejudice is a romantic period film based on Jane Austen's novel of the same name. It follows the story of five sisters living in Georgian England. Jane, Elizabeth, Mary, Kitty, and Lydia Bennet's lives are turned upside down when Mr. Bingley and his best friend Mr. Darcy arrive in town.
Although it has been 15 years since the release of this movie, I watched it for the first time last month. I fell in love almost instantly. The atmosphere of the movie felt very real. The costume design really helped with the immersion. I feel that it is a movie that will have you captivated from beginning to end. An instant classic that everyone should watch.
-Vanessa Garcia
