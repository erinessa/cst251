Start-Up

Start-Up is a South Korean television series. The series revolves around a woman with aspirations of becoming a successful entrepreneur, her secret first love, and the man who is pretending to be her first love. It aired on tvN and Netflix simultaneously.
I am currently watching this tv show and I highly recommend it to anyone who is okay with reading subtitles. It feels like a breath of fresh air and an escape from reality. The cast was well chosen and I feel that their performances make the series engaging and bingeable. I will be finishing this series as soon as I am done with finals :)
-Vanessa Garcia
